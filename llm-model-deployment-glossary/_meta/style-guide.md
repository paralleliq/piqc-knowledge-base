# Glossary Style Guide

This document defines formatting, writing style, and content rules for all glossary entries.

The goal is:
- clarity over completeness  
- operational accuracy over theory  
- consistency across all terms  

---

## Standard Entry Template

Every term must follow this exact structure:

```markdown
## <Term>

**Definition**  
One-sentence, plain-language definition.

**Why it exists**  
What problem this concept solves or why it matters.

**Where in the stack**  
Execution | Serving | Control plane | GPU & Memory | Distributed | Observability | Reliability

**Key properties**
- 3–6 concise bullets
- Describe behavior, constraints, and scaling
- Focus on operational relevance

**Common pitfalls**
- 3–6 actionable failure modes or misunderstandings
- Must be concrete and experience-based

**Related terms**
- 4–6 closely related glossary entries

**In practice**  
1–2 sentences describing how this appears in real systems, tools, or incidents.
